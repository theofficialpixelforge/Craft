Google Calendar Integration

Craft requests read-only access to your Google Calendar to display your events alongside Craft's calendar. Here's exactly what we do and don't do:

  • We read your Google Calendar events to show them in the Craft calendar view.
  • We never write, create, modify, or delete any of your Google Calendar events.
  • We never store your calendar data on any server — events are fetched live and displayed in your browser only.
  • Your token is stored only in your browser's local storage and is never sent to our servers.
  • You can disconnect at any time using the disconnect button in the Calendar view.

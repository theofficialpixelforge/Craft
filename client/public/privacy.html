Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Craft ("we", "our", or "the app") is a workplace productivity tool for managing documents, calendars, leave tracking, and team communication. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use Craft.

Information We Collect

Craft operates primarily in your browser. The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your name and email address (used to personalise the app)
  • Documents, calendar events, and notes you create within the app
  • Leave and attendance records entered by you or your manager
  • Your role and account preferences

Google Calendar Integration

If you choose to connect your Google Calendar, Craft requests read-only access to your Google Calendar data using the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.readonly scope.

We use this access to:

  • Display your Google Calendar events alongside your Craft calendar

We do not:

  • Store your Google Calendar events on any server
  • Share your calendar data with any third party
  • Modify, create, or delete any Google Calendar events

Your Google OAuth access token is stored only in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to Craft's servers. You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time using the disconnect button in the Calendar view, which immediately removes the token from your browser.

Craft's use of Google user data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

Data Storage

All app data (documents, calendar events, leave records, and preferences) is stored in your browser's localStorage. Craft does not operate a database that stores your personal data on external servers beyond what is inherent in serving the web application.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. No analytics trackers or advertising networks are embedded in this application.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since data is stored in your browser, you can delete it at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site. To revoke Google Calendar access, visit your Google Account permissions page and remove Craft.

Children's Privacy

Craft is intended for use by adults in a workplace setting. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
